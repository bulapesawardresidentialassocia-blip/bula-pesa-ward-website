About Our Association

The Bula Pesa Ward Residents Association serves as the official representative body for all residents of Bula Pesa Ward in Isiolo County. Our mission is to promote the welfare, security, and development of our community.

Our Mission

To unite residents, address community concerns, and promote sustainable development in Bula Pesa Ward.

Our Vision

A harmonious, developed, and prosperous Bula Pesa Ward where every resident's voice is heard and valued.

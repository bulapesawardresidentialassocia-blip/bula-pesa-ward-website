Building a Safer, Stronger Bula Pesa

BPWRA is a registered community institution committed to protecting lives, strengthening infrastructure, and advancing sustainable development in Isiolo.

Who We Are

The Bula Pesa Ward Residents Association is a community-led organization dedicated to advancing public safety, environmental protection, and inclusive development. Through collective action, we are transforming Bula Pesa into a model ward defined by cooperation, resilience, and local leadership.

Our Impact

  • ✔ Repaired cemetery fence protecting community dignity
  • ✔ Constructed pedestrian bridge improving town access
  • ✔ Supported vulnerable families during emergencies
  • ✔ Led community clean-up initiatives

Over KSh 1.3 Million mobilized through community effort.

Flagship Initiative: River Marile Climate Resilience Project

For over a decade, River Marile has caused loss of life, destroyed property, and exposed residents to environmental and public health risks. BPWRA is leading a multi-stakeholder initiative to reduce flood risk, improve sanitation, and protect vulnerable households.

We invite partners, donors, and government agencies to join us in delivering lasting solutions.

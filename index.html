About Our Association

Our Mission & Vision

The Bula Pesa Ward Residents Association serves as the unified voice for all residents in our ward. Our mission is to improve the quality of life, advocate for community needs, and promote sustainable development in our area.

Established to address the common concerns of residents, we collaborate with local authorities, organize community initiatives, and work towards creating a safe, clean, and prosperous neighborhood for all.

We believe in the power of community collaboration and actively engage residents in decision-making processes that affect our ward's development and wellbeing.

Our Services & Activities

Community Advocacy

We represent residents' interests before local government and other stakeholders, advocating for better services, infrastructure, and policies that benefit our community.

Development Projects

We initiate and coordinate community development projects, including sanitation improvements, road maintenance, and public space enhancements.

Conflict Resolution

Our association provides mediation services to help resolve disputes among residents amicably, promoting harmony within the community.

River Marile Flood Risk Zone

Critical area requiring immediate intervention to protect vulnerable communities

Protecting Our Community From Climate Risks

The River Marile basin poses significant flood risks to over 1,200 households in Bula Pesa Ward. Our resilience project focuses on:

  • High-risk areas identified for immediate intervention
  • Early warning system installation points
  • Flood barrier construction zones
  • Emergency evacuation routes
  • Community resilience training locations

With climate change increasing flood frequency by 40% in the last decade, our work is more urgent than ever.
